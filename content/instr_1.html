In this experiment, you will see a series of shapes with stripes in different orientations and spatial frequencies (stripes with distances in between).
If you see a circle, regardless of the orientation and frequency of the stripes, please press G .
If you see a square, regardless of the orientation and frequency of the stripes, please withhold your response and do not press any keys.
However, when you see a square or circle surrounded by a colored frame, it is your job to figure out the correct key to press.
Notice the orientations and spatial Frequencies in these color-framed trials.
The correct answers come from X or M .
We will provide you feedback on your response.
In other words, you should figure out when to press A and when to press L through trial and error.
