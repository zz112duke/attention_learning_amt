Welcome!
In this experiment, you will see shapes with stripes in different orientations and spatial frequencies (stripes with different distances in between).
There are two ways we will present the shapes – unframed and framed.
If you just see a unframed circle, regardless of the orientation and frequency of the stripes, please press G on your keyboard.
If you just see a unframed square, regardless of the orientation and frequency of the stripes, please withhold your response and do not press any keys.
However, when you see a square or circle surrounded by a colored frame, it is your job to figure out the correct key to press.
Notice the orientations and spatial frequencies in these color-framed shapes. Remember that the correct answers come from the key X or M .
We will provide you feedback on your responses to these framed shapes.
In other words, you should figure out when to press X and when to press M through trial and error.
